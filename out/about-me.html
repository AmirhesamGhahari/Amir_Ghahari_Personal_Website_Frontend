With over seven years of professional experience across healthcare, research, and analytics environments, I have consistently delivered reliable, high-performance data systems that enable organizations to make informed, data-driven decisions. Throughout my career, I have worked as a critical bridge between backend engineering teams that generate large volumes of data and business, analytics, and operations teams that rely on accurate, timely insights. This position at the center of data flow has allowed me to develop a deep understanding of both technical constraints and business priorities, ensuring that data solutions are not only technically sound but also strategically valuable.
